WHO AM I?

That's a really weird question to ask myself, but I'll try to tell you a thing or two. I recently graduated from the University of Brighton, and my subject specialization was illustration. I am ambitious to pursue a career in publication design and look forward to displaying my creativity in my work. I tend to use a combination of traditional drawing methods and digital media to produce my work, using tools such as Procreate and Adbobe Suite.


Working on a project gives me a sense of satisfaction, and being able to apply creative design solutions for clients gives me joy. This portfolio is a sneak peek into a selected collection of my works that help illustrate some of the more cheeful designs from the last year, as well as featuring artworks from my old university projects and personal ongoing ones.
