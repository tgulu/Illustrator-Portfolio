WHO AM I?

That's a really weird question to ask myself, but I'll try to tell you a thing or two. I recently graduated from the University of Brighton, specialising in illustration. I am ambitious to pursue a career in publication design and look forward to displaying my creativity in my work. I tend to use a combination of traditional drawing methods and digital media to produce my work, using tools such as Procreate and Adobe Suite.


Working on a project gives me a sense of satisfaction, and being able to apply creative design solutions for clients brings me joy. This portfolio is a sneak peek into a selected collection of my works that illustrate some of the more cheerful designs from the last year, as well as featuring artworks from my old university projects and personal ongoing ones.

Illustrator and designer based in sunny Brighton, well sometimes sunny… Working on projects from packaging to illustrated books, this site is a glimpse into my work and my silly drawings. Enjoy!
